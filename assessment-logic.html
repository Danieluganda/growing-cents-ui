Assessment Logic

Understand how assessments are structured, scored, and how results affect subscription and access.

Overview

The Growing Cents Health & Maturity Assessment is designed to evaluate the performance and development of MSMEs and investment clubs through a structured questionnaire, scoring system, and results reporting.

Assessment Structure

  • The assessment consists of multiple sections covering key business areas.
  • Each section includes targeted questions that address critical success factors.
  • Questions use a mix of multiple choice, rating scales, and open-ended responses.

Scoring and Results

  • Responses are scored based on predefined criteria for each question.
  • Scores are aggregated per section and overall to provide an overall health score.
  • Scores determine maturity levels categorized as Basic, Standard, or Premium tiers.
  • Users can view their overall score immediately after assessment completion.

Subscription and Access Model

  • Completion of an assessment triggers a billing event for paid subscriptions.
  • Free users have limited access to reports and dashboards; paid subscribers have full access.
  • Each club account maintains access to paid assessment reports indefinitely.
  • A 7-day grace period is provided post-submission to allow users to edit their assessment before finalization.

Reporting and Follow-up

  • Detailed reports and dashboards are generated for paid subscriptions based on assessment results.
  • Users can download PDF versions of their reports or receive summaries via email.
  • Recommendations and action plans may be provided to guide MSME growth.
